EDUCATION

Education is the first and foremost right of every human being. Every female is also entitled to education. Education is a key that can be used unlock many oppurtunities. Economic problem can be solved trough education. An educated mother has skill, talent and self reliance to make her a better mother. An educated woman will be the one to teach her children about their rights and moral values. Educating girls give them a chance to make use of all their capabilities.They will become more confident and handle their families better in all area.If we wish to see our contry progress and develop, we need to educate our girl children.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

Women regardless of gender, must be given equal chances in all feilds. Women empowerment is an economic process that involves empowering Nigerian women as a poverty reduction measure. Women empowerment includes accepting women's points of view, making an effort to raise the status of women through awareness literacy and training. women empowerment can be done through governmental and non-governmental sponsored programmes.

FEMALE HEALTH CARE

Gender equality in health should mean that women and men, girls and boys are on equal footing to fully exercise their right and potential to be healthy,contribute to health development and benefit from outcomes. A girl's health is very important to everyone. A woman's health affects the health of her family. When a woman is healthy her family is healthy too.

ERADICATION OF CHILD MARRIAGE

Early child marriage is commonly practiced in the northern part of Nigeria.It involves forcing children under the age of eighteen into marriage. It is mostly done to the female children. It could make a girl child emotionally inbalanced. Child marriage could be eradicated through education,empowerment and creation of awareness to community leaders and parents on the dangers of forcing their girl child into marriage.
